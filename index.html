How to Play

Move: WASD / arrows / touch pad. Guard: Space or L.

Light punch: J. Power punch: K. Body punch: U. Slip: Shift.

In Open World, train at the gym to unlock new punches, then equip your J/K/U loadout. Jabs set up combos, power shots punish mistakes, and body shots drain stamina.

Guard blocks, but spends stamina and block. Slip at the right time to create counter opportunities.

Touch: use the left pad to move and the action buttons for punches, slip, and guard.
